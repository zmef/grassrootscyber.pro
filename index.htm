Cybersecurity. Community. Common Sense.

Grass Roots Cyber is an unincorporated nonprofit association dedicated to building digital resilience in local communities through open collaboration, practical education, and collective stewardship. We believe that cybersecurity is for everyone, and we aim to make that real — from libraries to school districts, from hackerspaces to farms.

Become a Member

Anyone can join Grass Roots Cyber by showing up, contributing, or simply aligning with our principles. We operate using Open Space Technology, which means leadership is shared, emergent, and self-organizing. You don’t need permission — just intention.

Sign the Statement of Organization

Organizational Partners

Organizations — schools, nonprofits, local governments, tech collectives, and more — are welcome to join. Simply appoint a Steward to participate on your behalf, align with our Statement of Organization, and sign publicly to affirm your partnership.

How to Join:
  • Review our Statement of Organization.
  • Appoint a Steward from your organization.
  • Sign as an organization via GitHub or hosted form.

Why Grass Roots?

We root ourselves in the commons — in the belief that care, collaboration, and practical knowledge grow stronger from the ground up. We’re building a federated, transparent, and regenerative network of security-aware humans and institutions who protect what matters most: each other.
